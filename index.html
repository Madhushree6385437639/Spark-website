About Us

Dedicated to Your Academic Excellence

Spark IIT Academy is a NEET and IIT entrance exam coaching centre with a strong back up of NCERT classes. Started with the idea of improving education at Hosur, Spark has grown from 5 students to 125 students (per year) in a span of 5 years. We are the first institute to build a separate campus for coaching (6000 sqft). Please see spark as an opportunity to improve your thinking skills and your abilities as a student beyond your academics.

The main thing we are proud of is the change that we bring in the students, where the students shift their focus from marks to understanding the subject. When the understanding is good, marks are bound to come. Our results speak of our claims (max NEET and IIT-mains pass percent and max district ranks).

  • Personalized attention for every student
  • Focus on student performance & discipline
  • Regular parent-teacher interactions
